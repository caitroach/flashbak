# super janky installation instructions
I refuse to pay $99 for an apple developer license, so you can sideload it... :3

**For macOS or windows:**
1. Install AltServer from https://altstore.io/
2. Turn on "developer mode" in iphone settings
3. Connect your iphone to your computer via USB
4. Use altserver to install altstore on your phone
5. Sign in on your computer and phone with your apple ID
6. download the .ipa file in this main branch and send it to your iphone (I personally used Notes)
7. sideload it as an app in AltStore
8. Open as an app

**For Linux:**
1. Borrow your friend's macbook or windows computer
